Menifee book donation FAQ

Do you pick up book donations in Menifee?

Yes. Menifee is one of our regular service areas. We do free book donation pickups across Menifee Lakes, Sun City, Audie Murphy Ranch, Heritage Lake, Quail Valley, The Lakes, and everywhere in ZIPs 92584, 92585, and 92586.

Do you pick up in Sun City specifically?

Yes, and often. Sun City is a major part of our Menifee route — we work with seniors, families, downsizers, and estate handlers in Sun City regularly. Many of our Sun City pickups are downsizing or estate situations.

How fast can you come to Menifee?

Most Menifee pickups happen within 3 to 5 days. We'll text you a confirmed window within 24 hours of your request.

What Menifee ZIP codes do you cover?

All three — 92584, 92585, and 92586 — plus the edges into Romoland, Quail Valley, and Sun City proper.

Do I need to be home for the pickup?

Not always. If the boxes are accessible (driveway, porch, or garage with a gate code), we can do contactless pickups. Just text us instructions when you confirm.

Do you take whole estate libraries in Menifee?

Yes — we handle full estate libraries regularly, especially in Sun City and Heritage Lake. For larger jobs, see our [estate library cleanout page](/estate-library-cleanout.html).

What can't you take?

Moldy or water-damaged books, books with major damage, magazines, newspapers, encyclopedia sets, and old media like VHS tapes.
