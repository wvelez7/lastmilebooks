Not books? Here's where to donate other items in Murrieta

We only pick up books — but we know people searching for donation pickup usually have a mix of stuff. Here are the best local options for other donation types:

Furniture, appliances, and large items

Habitat for Humanity ReStore — free furniture pickup in the Temecula/Murrieta area. Call ahead to schedule. They take couches, tables, working appliances, and building materials.

Clothing, small housewares, and general donations

Goodwill Southern California and Salvation Army both do scheduled pickups for clothing, kitchenware, and small household items. Salvation Army also takes larger furniture in many cases.

Working electronics and computers

Goodwill and local recycling centers accept working electronics. For non-working, check Riverside County's e-waste program.
