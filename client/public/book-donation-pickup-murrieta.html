Murrieta book donation FAQ

Do you pick up book donations in Murrieta?

Yes — Murrieta is one of our core service areas. We do free book donation pickups across all of Murrieta, including Murrieta Hot Springs, The Colony, Copper Canyon, Greer Ranch, California Oaks, and Mapleton.

Is the pickup really free?

Yes, completely free. No pickup fee, no fuel surcharge, no minimums beyond a single box. We come to you, load the books from your driveway or garage, and leave.

How fast can you pick up in Murrieta?

Murrieta is close to our home base in Temecula, so most Murrieta pickups happen within 2 to 4 days of your request. We'll text you a confirmed window within 24 hours.

What ZIP codes do you cover in Murrieta?

Both Murrieta ZIPs — 92562 and 92563. If you're right on the edge of Wildomar, Menifee, or French Valley, we still come.

What books do you accept?

Almost any reading book in good condition — fiction, nonfiction, paperbacks, hardcovers, kids' books, cookbooks, recent textbooks, and most genres. We do not take moldy, water-damaged, or heavily damaged books, magazines, or encyclopedia sets.

Do I need to box the books up?

Boxed is easiest, but not required. Bags, bins, or stacks on a porch all work — just have them ready when we arrive.

Can I get a receipt for my donation?

We can provide a pickup confirmation showing the date and the items collected. We don't make claims about tax treatment — please check with your tax advisor about how donations may apply to your situation.
