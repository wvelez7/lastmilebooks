Palm Springs book donation FAQ

Do you really come all the way out to Palm Springs?

Yes. We run a regular weekly route through Palm Springs and the Coachella Valley. The pickup itself is free — but because it's about an hour drive from our Temecula base, we batch pickups into one day per week.

How does the schedule work for Palm Springs?

Once you request a pickup, we'll tell you which day that week we're in the Coachella Valley. We ask for a few days of flexibility on timing. We confirm a window within 24 hours.

What Coachella Valley cities do you serve?

Palm Springs, Palm Desert, Rancho Mirage, Cathedral City, La Quinta, Indian Wells, and Desert Hot Springs. Indio and points east depend on the route — let us know when you schedule.

Is there a minimum for Palm Springs?

Because of the drive, we ask for at least 3 boxes for the Palm Springs route. Smaller loads are welcome anywhere closer to our Temecula base.

Do you do estate or downsizing pickups in Palm Springs?

Yes, all the time. Many of our Palm Springs pickups are larger estate libraries, snowbird downsizing, or seasonal residence cleanouts. See our [estate library cleanout page](/estate-library-cleanout.html) for full-library service.

What books can I donate?

Most reading books in good condition. We can't take moldy, water-damaged, magazines, encyclopedias, or heavily damaged books. We don't need everything to be perfect — just readable.

Can I get a receipt?

We can give you a pickup confirmation showing the date and items collected. We don't make claims about tax treatment — please check with your tax advisor about how donations may apply to your situation.
